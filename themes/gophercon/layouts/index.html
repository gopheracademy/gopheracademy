Join us in Denver for GopherCon 2016

GopherCon It's the largest event in the world dedicated solely to the Go programming language. It's attended by the best and the brightest of the Go community. Join us as 1,500 gophers converge on Denver, Colorado for 3 full days of talks, events, training & hacking.

GopherAcademy

GopherAcademy is an organization created by Go enthusiasts for the promotion of the Go programming language through training, community events, and more. GopherAcademy consists of community volunteers who help to coordinate conferences, educational and promotional blog posts, and online communities.

GopherCon is our yearly conference for the Go language. Last year, GopherCon was a sold out hit, so we've expanded our venue this year to accomodate twice the number of attendees.
